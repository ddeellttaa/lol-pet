## Visual Observations

- Base Syndra reads through a tall silver-white hair mass, angular black-purple crown, violet-black bodysuit, high jagged shoulder armor, and floating dark spheres.
- The pet version should keep one small dark sphere close to the hands/body as the signature prop; full-size splash orbs and detached space effects would clutter a 192x208 cell.
- Use saturated green chroma key. Avoid blue or purple chroma keys because Syndra relies on violet magic and dark purple clothing.
- Model-viewer reference is recorded for the base skin id, but the Data Dragon splash/loading/icon are the local visual authorities for this run.
