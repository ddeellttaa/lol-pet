## Visual Anchors

- Side-swept black hair and pale, silent face.
- Purple shoulder mantle over a dark navy-black outfit.
- Moon-silver armor and crescent weapon shapes.
- Restrained cyan-blue Lunari glow with occasional magenta-pink weapon energy.

## Risk Notes

- Do not represent Alune as a second sprite; she is only part of the lore cue.
- Do not draw all five weapons at once. One compact crescent weapon plus a tiny off-hand hint is enough.
- Avoid UI metaphors for Phase or the weapon queue; use body pose and a tucked weapon silhouette.
- Keep moonlight effects attached to the body or weapon so transparent extraction stays clean.
