## Visual Observations

- Base skin reads as an upright crocodilian Ascended warrior with a long snout, orange eyes, teal-green scaled skin, armored head/shoulders, and a thick curved tail.
- The headgear and shoulder armor are chunky gray stone/metal plates with small green gem accents; these should simplify into a few readable plates, not fine filigree.
- The weapon is a large double-ended crescent blade/polearm held horizontally in the model and as a sweeping cleaver in splash art. Keep it attached to the body silhouette.
- The silhouette needs the crocodile head, hunched brawler torso, heavy clawed hands/feet, tail, and oversized crescent blade more than exact armor patterning.
- Avoid turning him into a generic dinosaur, dragon, lizard, or human knight; the long crocodile snout, orange eyes, tail, and crescent blade are the identity anchors.
