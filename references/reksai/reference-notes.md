## Visual Read

- Rek'Sai is a low, quadrupedal Void predator, not an upright armored warrior.
- The strongest silhouette anchors are the tall central forehead horn, sweeping purple side mandibles, broad blue-violet armored foreclaws, hunched back plates, dark hind claws, and pale lower fangs.
- The base palette reads as deep void purple, saturated cyan-blue carapace plates, magenta/pink horn and mandible accents, dark claw tips, and small pale yellow-white teeth.
- The face should feel eyeless or nearly eyeless. Avoid giving her a cute mammal face, human eyes, or a dragon snout.
- She should remain compact and pet-sized: simplify the many plates into a few chunky armor shapes.

## Common Misreads To Avoid

- Generic dragon, beetle, scorpion, dinosaur, crab, or dog-like monster.
- Upright humanoid posture, armor clothing, weapon props, wings, saddle, or rider.
- Sand scenery, tunnel openings as separate props, loose purple energy clouds, speed lines, shadows, and detached earth chunks.
- Pool Party, Blackfrost, Elderwood, Eternum, or Primordian skin colors.
